This website should work fine with Firefox (the browser I am using!) and Chrome on the desktop. There may be some glitches for the Microsoft browsers (Internet Explorer and Edge), but generally they will not affect most important contents on this site. Other browsers and platforms are not tested, though.

The map in the background is the 1:5,000,000 topographic lunar map in gradient tint. It is made public by Lunar and Planetary Institute on this page.

In accordance with the 1960's style map, the main menu of this website uses a geometric font, Glacial Indifference designed by Hanken Design Co.. And the body text uses a modern grotesque font, Carrois Gothic designed by Ralph Oliver du Carrois. Both are open fonts.

As a common practice of modern webpage design, I use jQuery made by jQuery Foundation to realize several necessary functions of this website.

Due to some reasons, I use a Chinese CDN service, bootCDN, to guarantee the loading speed of this site from mainland China. This service may make this site a little slower when it is visited from outside mainland China.

The texture under my portrait is obtained from the website "Transparent Textures", and the author of this pattern is John Burks.

I thank all the authors and suppliers of the resources used in this site. The resources used in the Chinese pages are cited separately in the Chinese.
