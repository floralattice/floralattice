I am currently a PhD student in finance at School of Business and Economics, Maastricht University, the Netherlands. I expect to receive my degree in 2017 and am now seeking a job position.

My research focuses on the pricing of commodities in financialized markets and the application of structural time series models in financial topics. I am also interested in the regulation of the banking industry and financial stability.

I got my bachelor's degrees in statistics and accounting in 2011 and master's degree in international economics in 2013, all of which are from Wuhan University, P. R. China.

The correct spelling of my name is Háng Sūn, but the tone marks (diacritics) are often omitted.
